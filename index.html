Our Story

Built on Craft. Driven by Pride.

Aurum Cuts was born from a simple belief — that every man deserves a barbershop experience that matches his standard. We don't just cut hair. We shape confidence.

Located in the heart of Manhattan, our shop blends old-school barbering tradition with modern luxury. Every detail, from our tools to our towels, is chosen with intention.
